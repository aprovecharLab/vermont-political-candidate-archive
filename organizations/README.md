This folder contains primary-source platform and program documents for five socialist and progressive political organizations, as part of the [Vermont Political Candidate Archive](https://github.com/aprovecharLab/vermont-political-candidate-archive).

> **Note:** The Social Democrats, USA (SDUSA) is referenced in the comparative analysis for historical context, but no platform document is included here as the organization is effectively dormant. Its successor lineage traces back to the Socialist Party of America (1901–1972).

## Comparative Analysis

The [`comparative_analysis.md`](./comparative_analysis.md) file in this folder provides a side-by-side comparison of the following organizations:

- **Vermont Progressive Party (VPP)** — a registered state political party in Vermont (founded 1981)
- **Democratic Socialists of America (DSA)** — the largest socialist organization in the United States (founded 1982; ~120,000+ members)
- **Social Democrats of America (SDA)** — a 501(c)(4) educational/advocacy group aligned with the Socialist International (incorporated 2021)
- **Socialist International (SI)** — a worldwide federation of social democratic, socialist, and labour parties (re-established 1951)
- **Social Democrats, USA (SDUSA)** — historically active social democratic organization; direct successor to the Socialist Party of America (renamed 1972; now dormant)

The analysis draws directly on the platform documents in this folder where available, supplemented by web research for organizational history, membership data, and current status.

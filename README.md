# Vermont Political Candidate Archive

A public archive of candidate positions, platform comparisons, and ideological alignment analyses for Vermont state-wide and local elections.

## Purpose

This repository exists to provide Vermont voters, journalists, organizers, and researchers with a centralized, publicly accessible, and version-controlled record of where candidates stand on the issues. All analyses are intended to be factual, sourced, and non-partisan in method — presenting candidates' stated positions accurately and comparing them against the published platforms of relevant political organizations where useful.

## Scope

- **Geographic scope:** State of Vermont — statewide offices (Governor, Lieutenant Governor, Attorney General, Treasurer, Secretary of State, Auditor), State Senate, State House, and selected local races (mayoral, city council, selectboard).
- **Temporal scope:** Current and future election cycles, with retrospective analyses added where relevant.
- **Issue scope:** All policy areas addressed by candidates, including but not limited to: housing, healthcare, education, climate and environment, economy and labor, reproductive rights, immigration, criminal justice, taxation, and democratic reform.

## Repository Structure

| Folder | Contents |
|--------|----------|
| `elections/` | Analyses organized by election year, then by office, then by locality where applicable |

## How to Read These Documents

Each analysis is a standalone Markdown file (`.md`) that renders automatically when viewed on GitHub. Files include:

- An **introduction** establishing the context of the race
- **Candidate backgrounds** with relevant biographical and professional details
- **Platform comparisons** organized by issue area
- **Alignment analyses** comparing candidate positions against published platforms of relevant political organizations (DSA, SDA, SI, SDUSA), where applicable
- **Side-by-side summary tables** for quick reference
- **Sources** listing all materials referenced in the analysis

Analyses reflect candidate positions as of the date noted on each document. Campaign positions may evolve. Always verify current details at candidate websites and verified social media accounts.

## Methodology

Analyses are compiled from publicly available sources, including:

- Candidate campaign websites and published platform pages
- Reporting from Vermont media outlets (Vermont Public, VTDigger, Seven Days, WCAX, Valley News, Vermont Business Magazine, local newspapers)
- Candidate social media accounts and public statements
- Candidate forum recordings and transcripts
- Ballotpedia and other electoral reference sites
- Published platforms of political organizations referenced for alignment comparisons (DSA, SDA, SI, SDUSA)

Where information is limited or unavailable, documents explicitly note gaps rather than inferring positions. Where sources conflict, both are cited with the discrepancy noted.

No candidate or campaign has editorial input into these analyses. This is an independent project.

## Disclaimer

This project is an independent civic resource. It is not affiliated with any campaign, party, or political organization. Analyses represent the compiler's best effort to accurately present publicly available information. Errors are possible. Always consult primary sources before making voting decisions.
